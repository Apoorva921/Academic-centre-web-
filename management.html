Welcome to the Management Program at IIT Kanpur

The Management program at IIT Kanpur is designed to cultivate leaders who blend technical expertise with strategic management skills. With a curriculum that bridges the gap between engineering and business, the program empowers students to excel in a variety of industries.

  • Marketing and Consumer Behavior

  • Entrepreneurship and Innovation
  • Human Resource Management
  • Strategic Management and Leadership
